<p><p>The Dry Sorbent Injection (DSI) System is an advanced emissions control technology primarily used in power plants and industrial facilities to reduce sulfur dioxide (SO2) and other pollutants from flue gas emissions. This system utilizes dry sorbents, typically materials like sodium bicarbonate or hydrated lime, which are injected into the flue gas stream. The sorbents react with the pollutants, resulting in byproducts that can be easily removed, thereby enhancing compliance with environmental regulations.</p><p>The Dry Sorbent Injection (DSI) System Market is expected to experience significant growth, projected at a CAGR of 4.7% during the forecast period.
