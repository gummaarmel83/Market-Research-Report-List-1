<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallic paint/coating market is primarily divided into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based metallic paints offer durability and a smooth finish, ideal for industrial applications, as they provide excellent adhesion and resistance to chemicals. However, they can emit volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Conversely, water-based metallic coatings are more environmentally friendly, featuring lower VOC levels and easier cleanup, making them suitable for residential and commercial use. Both types serve different aesthetic and functional needs in the coatings industry.</p></p>
