<p><ul><li>Needle</li><li>Needleless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bone Preparation Kit market is segmented into two main types: needle and needleless kits. Needle kits utilize traditional needles for extracting bone marrow or aspirating bone, allowing for precise procedures but often requiring more invasive techniques. In contrast, needleless kits employ advanced technologies, such as jet injection systems, minimizing discomfort and reducing infection risks by eliminating the need for needles. Both types cater to different surgical needs, with needleless options gaining popularity for their convenience and safety advantages.</p></p>
