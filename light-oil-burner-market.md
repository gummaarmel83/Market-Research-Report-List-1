<p><p>The light oil burner market comprises three primary types: Single Stage Burners, Two Stage Burners, and Modulating Burners. Single Stage Burners operate at a fixed output, providing simple and efficient heating for smaller applications. Two Stage Burners offer two output levels, allowing for moderate flexibility and improved efficiency in varying heating demands. Modulating Burners continuously adjust fuel and air mixture to match heat output precisely, ensuring optimal efficiency and minimal emissions, making them ideal for larger, more dynamic applications.</p></p>
