<p><p>The Synthetic O-Cresol market is categorized into four types based on the number and position of hydroxyl groups on the aromatic ring: 1N, 2N, 3N, and 4N. 1N o-cresol contains one hydroxyl group at the first position, while 2N has it at the second position, 3N at the third, and 4N at the fourth position.
