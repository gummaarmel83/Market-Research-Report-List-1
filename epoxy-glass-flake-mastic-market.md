<p><strong>The Epoxy Glass Flake Mastic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-drying Type</li><li>Drying Type</li><li>Radiation Curable Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Epoxy Glass Flake Mastic market is classified into three main types based on curing mechanisms. The self-drying type cures at ambient temperatures, providing ease of application without additional heat. The drying type relies on external heat sources to accelerate the curing process, enhancing durability and adhesion. Lastly, the radiation curable type uses UV or electron beam irradiation for rapid curing, allowing for a quick finish and improved performance. Each type serves different industrial applications based on performance requirements.</p></p>
