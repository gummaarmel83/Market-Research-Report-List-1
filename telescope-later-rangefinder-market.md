<p><strong>The Telescope Later Rangefinder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PhaseMethodTelemeter</li><li>PulseMethodTelemeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Telescope Laser Rangefinder market is divided into two primary types based on operational methods: Phase Method Telemeter and Pulse Method Telemeter. </p><p>Phase Method Telemeters utilize continuous wave laser signals to measure distances by analyzing the phase shift of the reflected light. This method is known for its precision and ability to function effectively over long distances. </p><p>On the other hand, Pulse Method Telemeter emits pulses of laser light and calculates the time it takes for the pulse to return. This approach is generally faster and effective for shorter distances.</p></p>
