<p><ul><li>Shrink Films</li><li>Stretch Films</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LDPE packaging market encompasses various types, notably shrink films and stretch films. Shrink films are designed to tightly wrap around products, providing protection and tamper-proofing when heat is applied. They are commonly used for bundling items and retail packaging. Stretch films, on the other hand, are elastic and used to secure pallet loads, ensuring stability during transportation. Both types offer excellent moisture resistance, making them ideal for various industries, including food, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods.</p></p>
