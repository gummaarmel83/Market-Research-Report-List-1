<p><p>Integrated Workplace Management System (IWMS) software is available in two primary market types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises IWMS is installed locally within an organization's infrastructure, offering greater control over data security, customization, and integration with existing systems. In contrast, cloud-based IWMS is hosted on external servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and easier access from anywhere with an internet connection. Cloud solutions often come with lower upfront costs, automated updates, and reduced IT maintenance, catering to diverse organizational needs.</p></p>
