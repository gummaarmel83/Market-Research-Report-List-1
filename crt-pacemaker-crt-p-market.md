<p><p>The CRT Pacemaker (CRT-P) market encompasses various types of devices designed to manage heart rhythm disorders. Extracorporeal portable pacemakers are external devices used temporarily for patients needing immediate stabilization. Implanted pacemakers are surgically placed within the body for long-term heart rhythm management. Semi-buried pacemakers offer a hybrid approach, partially under the skin and providing benefits of both implanted and external devices. Each type serves specific patient needs based on condition severity and treatment duration.</p></p>
