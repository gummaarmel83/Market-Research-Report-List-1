<p><p>The railway power supply systems market comprises several types, including Direct Power Supply Systems, which provide electricity directly from the grid to trains. BT (Bottom Transmission) Power Supply Mode involves distributing power from below the rail tracks, while AT (Above Transmission) Power Supply Mode delivers electricity from overhead lines. Coaxial Cable Power Supply Mode uses coaxial cables for efficient power distribution alongside tracks. Other market types may include alternative energy sources and hybrid systems to enhance efficiency and sustainability in rail transport.</p></p>
