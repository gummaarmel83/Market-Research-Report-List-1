<p><strong>The Modified Linseed Oils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stand Oil</li><li>Boiled Linseed oil</li><li>Raw Linseed oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modified linseed oils market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Stand Oil**: Produced by heating raw linseed oil, this type has a thick consistency and excellent drying properties, making it ideal for paints and varnishes. </p><p>2. **Boiled Linseed Oil**: This oil is treated with heat and additives, enhancing its drying time and durability, commonly used in wood finishes and as a binder in oil-based paints.</p><p>3. **Raw Linseed Oil**: The least processed, this oil maintains its natural properties but takes longer to dry, used primarily for wood treatment and as an artist's medium.</p></p>
