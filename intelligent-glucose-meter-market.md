<p><p>The Intelligent Glucose Meter market encompasses two primary types: portable and continuous monitoring devices. Portable meters are compact and user-friendly, allowing patients to measure blood glucose levels on-the-go, often requiring a blood sample from a fingertip. Continuous monitoring systems, on the other hand, utilize sensors that provide real-time glucose readings without the need for frequent finger pricks, enhancing convenience and day-to-day management for individuals with diabetes. Both types aim to improve patient outcomes through better glucose management.</p></p>
