<p><strong>The Underground Mining Diamond Drilling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary Drilling</li><li>Wireline Drilling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underground mining diamond drilling market is primarily categorized into two types: rotary drilling and wireline drilling. Rotary drilling involves the continuous rotation of a drill bit to extract core samples, allowing for effective exploration of mineral deposits. In contrast, wireline drilling utilizes a cable to retrieve core samples quickly and efficiently, minimizing downtime and enhancing data collection. Both methods are essential for assessing underground resources, ensuring operational efficiency, and supporting informed decision-making in mining exploration projects.</p></p>
