<p><p>The wheeled crane market includes two primary types: Cross-Country Cranes and All-Road Cranes. Cross-Country Cranes are designed for off-road mobility, featuring large tires that allow them to operate efficiently on rugged terrains. They are ideal for construction sites with challenging access. In contrast, All-Road Cranes are engineered for versatility, capable of navigating both urban environments and rough landscapes. These cranes combine road travel efficiency with off-road capabilities, making them suitable for a wide range of construction and lifting applications.</p></p>
