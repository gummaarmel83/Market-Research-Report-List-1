<p><p>Wind vane sensors are essential for measuring wind direction and are available in two main types: wired and wireless. Wired wind vane sensors connect directly to a data logging system, offering reliable performance and real-time data transmission but with limitations on placement due to cable constraints. In contrast, wireless wind vane sensors utilize radio frequency signals to transmit data, providing greater flexibility in installation and ease of maintenance, making them increasingly popular for remote monitoring applications in various industries.</p></p>
