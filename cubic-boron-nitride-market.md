<p><p>The cubic boron nitride (CBN) market encompasses two primary types: CBN monocrystalline and CBN micro mist. CBN monocrystalline consists of single, large crystals known for their superior hardness, making them ideal for precision machining and grinding applications in hard materials. In contrast, CBN micro mist consists of finely divided particles, often used for coatings and in applications requiring enhanced lubrication and thermal stability. Both types cater to specific industrial needs, enhancing cutting efficiency and extending tool life in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
