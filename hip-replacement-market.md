<p><p>The hip replacement market encompasses several types of implants designed for specific surgical needs. Total hip replacement implants involve replacing both the femoral head and acetabulum, providing comprehensive relief for severe arthritis or fractures. Partial hip replacement implants, often used for specific fractures, replace only the femoral head. Hip resurfacing implants preserve more bone and are suitable for younger patients. Revision hip replacement implants are essential for patients needing replacement of failed previous surgeries, addressing complications or wear of original implants.</p></p>
