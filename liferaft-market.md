<p><ul><li>Foldable Liferaft</li><li>Un-foldable Liferaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liferaft market is categorized into two main types: foldable and un-foldable liferafts. Foldable liferafts are compact, lightweight, and can be easily stored on vessels, making them ideal for emergency evacuation in limited spaces. They inflate rapidly and are designed for portable use. In contrast, un-foldable liferafts, often more robust, are typically larger and are permanently installed on ships. They provide greater capacity and durability, catering to larger crews and extended survival situations, ensuring safety during maritime emergencies.</p></p>
