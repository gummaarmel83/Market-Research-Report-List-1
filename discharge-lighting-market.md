<p><p>The discharge lighting market encompasses various types of gas discharge lamps. Low-pressure gas discharge lamps, such as fluorescent lamps, operate at low pressures, producing soft light ideal for general illumination. High-pressure discharge lamps include metal halide and high-pressure sodium lamps, delivering bright light for outdoor and industrial applications. Super high-pressure gas discharge lamps, often used in specialized settings like stadiums or large venues, emit intense light and have higher efficiency, catering to demanding lighting needs.</p></p>
