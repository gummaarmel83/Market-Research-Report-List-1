<p><strong>The Cereal Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cross-flow</li><li>Down-stream</li><li>Mixed-flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cereal dryer market is categorized into three main types: cross-flow, down-stream, and mixed-flow. Cross-flow dryers facilitate airflow perpendicular to the material's movement, enhancing moisture removal through increased surface contact. Down-stream dryers direct the airflow in the same direction as the material, promoting uniform drying and efficient energy use. Mixed-flow dryers combine both methods, allowing for flexibility and optimization of drying processes. Each type caters to different operational needs and product characteristics within the cereal processing industry.</p></p>
