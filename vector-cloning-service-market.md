<p><p>The Vector Cloning Service market is divided into two primary types: Viral Vectors and Non-Viral Vectors. Viral vectors utilize modified viruses to deliver genetic material into cells, leveraging their natural ability to infect and integrate DNA, making them highly efficient for gene therapy applications. Non-viral vectors, on the other hand, include methods like plasmid DNA, liposomes, and nanoparticles, which avoid using viruses, making them safer and easier to manipulate. Both types play crucial roles in research and therapeutic development.</p></p>
