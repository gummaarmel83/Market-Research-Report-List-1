<p><ul><li>Analog Signal Booster</li><li>Digital Signal Booster</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile signal booster market is primarily divided into two types: analog signal boosters and digital signal boosters. Analog signal boosters amplify incoming signals from cell towers and transmit them directly to devices, providing a simpler solution with limited frequency handling. In contrast, digital signal boosters utilize advanced technology to process and amplify signals, allowing for better management of multiple frequencies and enhanced performance in complex environments. Both types aim to improve mobile connectivity in areas with weak signals.</p></p>
