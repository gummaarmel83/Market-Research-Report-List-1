<p><strong>The VOC Emissions Detection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID Detector</li><li>Semiconductor Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC emissions detection market primarily features two types of technologies: PID (Photoionization Detector) and semiconductor detectors. PID detectors use ultraviolet light to ionize VOCs, making them highly sensitive and effective for detecting a wide range of organic compounds, even at low concentrations. Semiconductor detectors rely on changes in electrical conductivity when VOCs interact with the sensor surface, offering cost-effective solutions, albeit with slightly lower sensitivity and specificity compared to PID detectors. Both technologies are critical for environmental monitoring and industrial safety.</p></p>
