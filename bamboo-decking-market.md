<p><p>The bamboo decking market offers various types, each suited for specific needs. Solid bamboo decking features dense, solid strips of bamboo, providing durability and natural aesthetics. Engineered bamboo decking combines layers of bamboo with adhesives, enhancing stability and resistance to moisture. Strand woven bamboo decking is made by compressing bamboo fibers, resulting in a highly durable and dense product that mimics hardwood. Other bamboo decking options may include composites or treated varieties, designed for unique applications and environmental conditions.</p></p>
