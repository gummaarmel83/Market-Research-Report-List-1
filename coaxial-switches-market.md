<p><p>Coaxial switches are essential in RF signal routing, featuring various types based on their switching capabilities. SPnT (Single Pole n Throw) switches enable a single input to connect to multiple outputs. SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw) switches allow one input to connect to either of two outputs, while DPDT (Double Pole Double Throw) switches manage two separate inputs and outputs simultaneously. Other variants may include multi-position switches or programmable types, catering to specific applications across telecommunications, broadcasting, and research sectors.</p></p>
