<p><p>The foam board insulation market comprises three main types: EPS, XPS, and polyurethane foam boards. EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) offers good insulation at a lower cost, making it popular for various applications. XPS (Extruded Polystyrene) provides higher durability and moisture resistance, ideal for demanding environments like basements. Polyurethane foam boards deliver superior thermal performance and are often used in high-efficiency constructions. Each type serves distinct needs based on factors such as cost, insulation effectiveness, and moisture resistance.</p></p>
