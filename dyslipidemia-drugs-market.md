<p><strong>The Dyslipidemia Drugs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Statins</li><li>Cholesterol Absorption Inhibitors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dyslipidemia drugs are categorized primarily into three types: statins, cholesterol absorption inhibitors, and others. Statins, the most common class, reduce cholesterol production in the liver, effectively lowering LDL levels. Cholesterol absorption inhibitors work by decreasing the absorption of cholesterol from the intestines. The "Others" category includes a variety of agents, such as PCSK9 inhibitors and fibrates, which target different pathways to manage lipid levels. Together, these therapies aim to reduce cardiovascular risks associated with dyslipidemia.</p></p>
