<p><p>The E-Glass fiberglass yarns market encompasses two primary types: "Fiber Yarn" and "Fiber Roving." Fiber yarn refers to continuous strands of fiberglass filaments that are woven or knitted into fabrics, providing strength and durability for various applications. Fiber roving, on the other hand, consists of untwisted bundles of fiber used primarily for composites, enabling easy handling and application in processes like winding and molding. Both types serve essential roles in industries such as construction, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
