<p><p>Antibody Drug Conjugates (ADCs) are innovative therapeutic agents that combine monoclonal antibodies with cytotoxic drugs, allowing for targeted cancer treatment. This targeted approach enhances treatment efficacy and reduces side effects, driving significant interest and investment in ADCs.</p><p>The ADC market is poised for substantial growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 10.9% during the forecast period.
