<p><p>The geosynthetics market encompasses various types, including geotextiles, geomembranes, geogrids, and others. Geotextiles are permeable fabrics used in soil filtration and stabilization. Geomembranes are impermeable membranes utilized for containment applications, such as landfill liners. Geogrids are structures designed to reinforce soil and improve load distribution in civil engineering projects. Other products in this market can include geonets, geofoams, and geocells, which support various applications in construction, environmental protection, and infrastructure development.</p></p>
